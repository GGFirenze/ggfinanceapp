Frequently asked questions

Can I try Pleo before I commit?
Absolutely! Every paid plan comes with a free 21-day trial. No credit card required to get started.
Can I change my plan later?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
Are there any hidden fees?
No hidden fees. The price you see is the price you pay. Card transactions in your local currency have no additional fees.
What currencies are supported?
Pleo cards work with Mastercard's global network, so you can pay in virtually any currency. Foreign exchange fees may apply for non-local currencies.
How does the Freshdesk chat support work?
You can reach our support team anytime through the chat widget on this page. Essential plan users get priority response times, and Enterprise customers have 24/7 dedicated support.
